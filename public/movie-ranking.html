Hereditary (2018)

is a psychological horror film directed by Ari Aster. The story centers around the Graham family, who begin to unravel after the death of their secretive matriarch, Ellen. Her daughter, Annie (Toni Collette), her husband Steve (Gabriel Byrne), and their children Peter (Alex Wolff) and Charlie (Milly Shapiro) start experiencing disturbing and increasingly terrifying occurrences. As they delve deeper into their familyâ€™s dark secrets, they uncover a sinister legacy that seems to be inherited. The film masterfully blends themes of grief, trauma, and the supernatural, creating a haunting and emotionally charged narrative.

The Exorcist (1973)

is a horror film directed by William Friedkin, based on the novel by William Peter Blatty. The story follows a 12-year-old girl named Regan MacNeil (Linda Blair) who begins exhibiting strange and disturbing behavior. Her mother, Chris (Ellen Burstyn), after exhausting all medical explanations, seeks the help of two priests, Father Merrin (Max von Sydow) and Father Karras (Jason Miller). They come to realize that Regan is possessed by a demonic entity, and they perform a harrowing exorcism to save her. The film is renowned for its intense and terrifying depiction of demonic possession and its impact on those involved.

Insidious (2010)

is a supernatural horror film directed by James Wan. The story revolves around the Lambert family, who move into a new home and soon experience disturbing events. When their young son, Dalton (Ty Simpkins), inexplicably falls into a coma, they realize he is trapped in a realm called "The Further," a dark and dangerous place inhabited by malevolent spirits. Desperate to save Dalton, the family enlists the help of paranormal experts, including a psychic named Elise (Lin Shaye). Together, they uncover the sinister forces at play and fight to bring Dalton back before it's too late. The film is known for its eerie atmosphere, jump scares, and compelling storytelling.
